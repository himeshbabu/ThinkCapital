Our Services

Custom Services & Solutions Built Specifically for Your Business

Since our inception in 2008, our emphasis has been on providing specialized, independent, and tailor-made solutions in the field of Corporate Finance, Insolvency and Operational Restructuring, Mergers and Acquisitions and Management Consultancy.

Corporate Finance

Comprehensive capital raising and financial advisory services.

We provide comprehensive capital raising and financial advisory services to support business growth, strategic expansion, and value creation. Our team works with both high-growth and established companies to raise capital for organic growth, acquisitions, and liquidity requirements. We also advise stressed and special situation companies on refinancing, structured funding, and strategic capital infusion.

With strong relationships across banks, financial institutions, private equity funds, venture capital investors, and special situation funds, we are well-positioned to facilitate efficient transaction execution. Our deep understanding of market dynamics and investor expectations enables us to structure solutions that are practical, timely, and aligned with client objectives.

Equity Advisory

We advise clients on raising equity capital across various stages of their business lifecycle. Our services include facilitating investments from angel investors and high net-worth individuals, venture capital and private equity funding, as well as strategic investments and PIPE transactions. We also support clients in accessing capital markets and exploring social impact funding opportunities, ensuring alignment with long-term growth strategies.

Debt Advisory

We offer tailored debt solutions to address diverse financing requirements. Our services cover growth and expansion capital, acquisition financing, and external commercial borrowings. We also specialize in advising on special situations, including restructuring and refinancing, along with structuring customized debt solutions and facilitating unsecured funding. Our approach focuses on optimizing capital structure while ensuring flexibility and sustainability for the business.

Mergers & Acquisitions Advisory

Strategic transactions supporting long-term value-driven outcomes.

We provide end-to-end mergers and acquisitions advisory services to corporates, supporting strategic transactions including acquisitions, divestitures, buyouts, strategic investments, and distressed sales. Our approach is grounded in a comprehensive evaluation of the business, industry dynamics, competitive landscape, financial performance, and relevant legal, regulatory, and accounting considerations—ensuring well-informed and value-driven outcomes.

Sell-Side Advisory

We act as a strategic partner throughout the divestment process, offering end-to-end support from transaction preparation to closure. Our role includes positioning the business, identifying and engaging with potential investors, facilitating negotiations, and coordinating with advisors and stakeholders. We provide objective guidance to help clients evaluate bids and achieve optimal value realization.

Distressed M&A Advisory

We assist companies in navigating distressed sale situations through structured and pragmatic strategies. Our services include identifying potential buyers such as ARCs and strategic investors, conducting valuation and synergy assessments, structuring transactions, and managing timelines. We also support resolution planning, buyout structuring, and post-transaction integration and turnaround initiatives.

Buy-Side Advisory

We support clients in executing inorganic growth strategies by identifying, evaluating, and executing suitable acquisition opportunities. Our process includes detailed due diligence, synergy assessment, and evaluation of commercial and non-commercial terms. We work closely with stakeholders to structure transactions that maximize value and ensure seamless execution.

Distressed & Special Situations Advisory

Comprehensive solutions for business revival and turnaround.

We provide comprehensive, end-to-end advisory solutions for businesses facing financial or operational stress, supporting them from early signs of distress through to revival and sustainable turnaround. Our approach integrates financial, operational, and strategic interventions to stabilize and reposition businesses effectively. Backed by a team of experienced former bankers and insolvency professionals, we bring deep execution capability and strong relationships with banks, financial institutions, special situation funds, and ARCs.

Insolvency & Bankruptcy Advisory

We offer end-to-end support across insolvency and resolution processes, including resolution plan preparation, interim and priority funding, and independent evaluation of resolution proposals. Our services extend to transaction structuring, turnaround advisory, and managing operations of stressed companies during the resolution phase. We also undertake CRO (Chief Restructuring Officer) roles and support M&A transactions within insolvency scenarios.

Operational Restructuring

We assist businesses in improving operational efficiency and restoring profitability through structured interventions. Our services include independent business reviews, process optimization, and identification of control gaps. We support implementation of resolution plans, facilitate turnaround funding, and enable induction of strategic partners. Our approach also focuses on cash flow management, performance improvement, and ongoing monitoring to ensure sustainable recovery.

Financial Restructuring

We advise on restructuring of capital structures to enhance financial stability and long-term viability. Our services include debt restructuring, refinancing, turnaround funding, and formulation of resolution plans. We also assist in resolution of stressed assets, strategic investments, change in management, distressed M&A, and sale of non-performing assets (NPAs), ensuring a holistic and value-maximizing approach.
