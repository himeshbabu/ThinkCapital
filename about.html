Who We Are

A value-driven independent corporate advisory firm with a commitment to leadership, integrity, and measurable results.

Our Story

Think Capital is a leading midmarket investment banking and corporate advisory firm built on a strong, value-based legacy.

We are an independent corporate advisory and consulting firm specializing in Corporate Finance, Insolvency and Operational Restructuring, Mergers and Acquisitions, and Management Consultancy services.

Our philosophy is simple: Focus + Expertise + Leadership. We strive to provide solutions that preserve and enhance value for our clients — from Corporates and Investors to Banks and Special Situation Funds.

Headquartered in Mumbai's financial district at Nariman Point, we serve businesses across India with a dedication to excellence and a commitment to long-term partnerships.

Our Mission

"To deliver exceptional, independent advisory that preserves and enhances value for every client we serve — grounded in integrity and driven by leadership."

Our Vision

"To become India's most trusted midmarket advisory firm by building lasting relationships and delivering measurable impact."
